What is the Talking Trees Jam?

Talking Trees Jam is a weekend workshop where participants will collaborate on interactive art installations that embody Colorado environmental data.

What you'll be able to create

We will provide art and craft materials along with electronics and other technologies.

All experience levels are welcome!

We understand that many people have not made interactive art installations before, and that's okay! We will be supplying beginner-friendly technology and materials that anyone can learn how to use, even if you think you might not be tech-savvy or creative.

When and where is it?

Talking Trees Jam is a two day event:
September 14 6pm-9pm
September 15 9am-9pm
Norlin Library M350B

What else you should know

Lunch and dinner will be provided on the second day of the event, September 15.
Projects will be temporarily installed in Norlin Library's STEAM Gallery.
